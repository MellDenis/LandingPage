Succulents Family

Also known as succulent plants, fleshy plants or plants with fleshy leaves, it must be borne in mind that they are not a family of plants, but we can find them in different families. Cacti, agaves or the Aloe genus are some examples of succulents. Succulents are hardy and easy to care for. The most important thing is the light, since they must receive it in abundance for a long time. However, it must be taken into account that some varieties do not tolerate too many hours of direct sunlight well, so we will place them in places where the sun affects several hours a day but also have a few hours of shade.
